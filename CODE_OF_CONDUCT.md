# Code of Conduct

This policy is intended for public repositories.

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior
Contributors are expected to:
- communicate respectfully and professionally
- assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people
- provide actionable technical feedback
- keep discussions focused on repository quality and user safety

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behavior is not accepted:
- harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks, intimidation, or threats
- trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith participation
- publishing private information without consent

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- issues and pull requests
- repository discussions and review comments
- other project-related communication channels

## Reporting
If you observe unacceptable behavior, report it through:
- email: contact@nandi.com.ar

Include context and links so maintainers can review quickly.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

Enforcement decisions are made to protect contributor safety and project health.
